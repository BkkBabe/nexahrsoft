🔧 Developer Instruction: MOM-Compliant Salary & Overtime Calculations (SG)
Objective
Ensure hourly rate, OT 1.5x, and OT 2.0x calculations fully comply with Singapore MOM Employment Act and match official MOM employment records.

1️⃣ Required Inputs (System Level)
Field	Description	Required
monthly_basic_salary	Basic monthly salary (exclude allowances)	✅
weekly_contract_hours	Contractual hours per week (e.g. 44)	✅
weekly_work_days	Days worked per week (e.g. 5.5)	Optional (display only)
⚠️ weekly_contract_hours must be mandatory.

2️⃣ Core Formula (DO NOT ALTER)
Hourly Basic Rate (MOM Formula)
hourly_rate =
(monthly_basic_salary × 12)
÷ (52 × weekly_contract_hours)
Overtime Rates
ot_1_5_rate = hourly_rate × 1.5
ot_2_0_rate = hourly_rate × 2.0

3️⃣ Expected Output (Example)
Input
monthly_basic_salary = 1400
weekly_contract_hours = 44
System Output (rounded to 2 decimals)
hourly_rate  = 7.34
ot_1_5_rate  = 11.01
ot_2_0_rate  = 14.68
✅ OT 1.5x must match MOM employment record ✅ OT 2.0x used for rest day / public holiday calculations

4️⃣ Mandatory System Rules
✔️ Auto-calculation
* Hourly rate must not be manually editable
* OT rates must update dynamically when:
    * Salary changes
    * Weekly hours change
✔️ Rounding
* Display values: 2 decimal places
* Internal calculation: full precision before rounding

5️⃣ Explicitly Disallowed Methods 🚫
Do NOT calculate using:
* 26 working days per month
* 30 / 31 calendar days
* 5-day work week assumption
* Fixed monthly hours (e.g. 208)
* User-entered hourly rate
Any of the above = MOM non-compliant

6️⃣ UI / UX Requirements
* Label hourly rate as: “Hourly Basic Rate (MOM-compliant)”
* Tooltip / helper text: Calculated using MOM formula: (Monthly Salary × 12) ÷ (52 × Weekly Hours)
* Display both:
    * OT 1.5x Rate
    * OT 2.0x Rate

7️⃣ Compliance Note (Important)
System calculations must match MOM Employment Terms printout exactly. Mismatch may result in:
* Underpayment claims
* MOM audit issues
* Backpay liabilities
